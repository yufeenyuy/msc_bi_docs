# CORPORATE GOVERNANCE OF IT, COMPLIANCE, AND LAW

## IT Governance Motivation and Challenges

**IT Governance**
<p>This is a component of a companys' entire *corporate governance program*. It is a requirment under many local and international laws, which is defined as *processes that ensure effective and efficient use of IT to enable an organization achieve its goals*. This definition is provided by Gartner and it is in a much greater part aligns with the defination provided by **Control Objective for Information and Related Technologies (COBIT)**. COBIT is an IT governance framework create by *Information Technology Audit and Control Association (ISACA)*. According to *ISO/IEC 38500:2015*, another widely accepted IT governance standard, IT governance is simply a *subset* of an organizations' corporate governance program.</p>

**Framework for IT Governance**
<p>
Supervising an organization whether their activities are compliant to rules and regulations by using and maintaining existing checklist is inefficient as this can create addtional work and affect the implementation of business objectives. To deal with this *challenge* and provide a methode for *impleanting and measuring IT governance initiatives*, as a component of corporate governance, Frameworks have been developed and made available. COBIT is one of the widely accepted IT governance frameworks which companies can implement.
</p>
